About Us

Hottest inyozi in Nakuru town!!

Founded in 2019 ,Clean Kuts Barbershop is an classic old shool Barbershop with new school twist,we speacialize in classic clean cuts ,hot towel shaves, and all modern men grooming services.We believe in an experience that feels authentic in its people, products, and, ambiance. We pride ourselves on a business model free from gimmicks; where a man can enjoy a classic place that helps restore his sense of self. Our passion for preserving pieces of history is expressed both physically: in our careful restoration of antique turn-of-the-century barber chairs and other furnishings, repurposing old salvaged materials in our architecture and design, and emotionally: in helping create a familiar environment that welcomes all ages from your son’s first haircut, to a hot lather, straight razor shave for your grandfather. These old-fashioned traditions at freshKuts Barbershop are carefully maintained with the art of true barbering and shaving, as well in our abundant selection of unique gentlemen’s grooming and specialty retail products. Our interest in urban renewal is valued through specifically serving the downtown business, residential, and tourist communities. So whether you work, live, or visit one of our cities, we offer you our hospitality, and genuinely appreciate the opportunity to serve you. “It pays to look well”
